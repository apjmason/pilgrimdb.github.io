The [Lahnhöhenweg][0] from Wetzlar (see [Elisabethpfad][1]) to Lahnstein on the Rhine has in addition been signed with scallop-shells. [Karl-Josef Schäfer][2] produces a guide.  
See also [lahn-camino.de][3].
