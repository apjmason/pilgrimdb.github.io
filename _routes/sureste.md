A route from Alicante, largely the same as the [Camino de Levante][0], which it joins at Albacete. From Medina del Campo it heads NW to the [Via de la Plata][1] at Benavente.  
The [Alicante Amigos site][2] includes a complete downloadable guide, and there is an online guide on [Mundicamino][3]. There is a detailed description for the route in Avila province on the [Avila Amigos site][4], and a description of the route in Valladolid province on the [Valladolid Amigos site][5].The latter also includes a link to the [Camino Sanabr√©s][6] at Sta Marta de Tera.
